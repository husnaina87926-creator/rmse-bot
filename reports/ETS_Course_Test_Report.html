2 · KAISE Test Kiya — Methodology

2.1 Objective coding (jo likha hai wahi code kiya)

  • Structure engine: mother-candle legs; retracement sirf course ke rules se confirm — 2 consecutive candle closes body ke paar, inside bars ignored, ya 1 engulfing close. Swings sirf confirmed retracements se.
  • BoS = candle close swing ke paar. Zone = retracement leg ki lowest-closing opposite candle (open→wick). MsS = protected high/low ke paar 2 closes.
  • OB/MB/FVG/CISD/MCOB — sab fixed, documented definitions se. Kuch tune nahi kiya (koi grid-search nahi) — jo pehli, imandaar interpretation thi wahi test hui.

2.2 Validity filters (course ke mutabiq "valid entry")

  • HTF alignment: trade sirf bade TF ki structure-direction mein (5m→1h, 15m→4h, 1h→1d, 4h→1d) — sirf COMPLETED HTF bars se (koi lookahead nahi)
  • Unmitigated: zone ka sirf PEHLA touch
  • Testing-candle rule, next-candle-open entry, SL = zone ka door wala kinara, TP = area-3

2.3 Honesty/hygiene rules (jo har test pe laazim thay)

Fees: 0.10%/side crypto · 0.01% gold (asli Binance/broker level)

4 · Q Fail Hua — Forensic Analysis

Har valid ETS entry pe humne likha ke aage kya hua (20,000+ entries):

  • Course ka RR wada sahi nikla — target aam taur pe ~2.2R door hota hai ✅
  • Magar price wahan sirf ~30% baar pahunchta hai; ~70% baar pehle SL lagta hai ❌
Math jo sab samjha deta hai: 0.30 × (+2.2R)  −  0.70 × (−1R)  ≈  0R
